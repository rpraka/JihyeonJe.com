Development of a fusion nano probe using affibody molecules to target cancer

A novel protein cage nanoparticles, encapsulin, is adequate for the construction of multifunctional drug delivery nanoplatform. Utilizing genetically engineered encapsulin, we develop a selective targeted drug delivery system.
