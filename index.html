I am an incoming Computer Science Ph.D. student at Stanford University. I received my Bachelor's degree in Biomedical Engineering, Electrical/Computer Engineering, and Computer Science from Duke University.

My passions lie at the intersection of geometry/structure processing, machine learning, healthcare, medicine, and engineering.
