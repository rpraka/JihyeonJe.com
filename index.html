About me

I am an undergraduate student at Duke University studying Biomedical Engineering and Electrical/Computer Engineering. My passions lie at the intersection of computational and data science, healthcare, medicine, and engineering. Some of my past projects have been focused on machine learning based biological and medical data analysis. I am currently involved in research under the Woo Center for Big Data and Precision Health on pathological health data analysis, am part of the Duke iGEM team, and am also working as a teaching assistant for the ECE department. In my free time, I like to read, run, explore good restaurants, and rewatch Gilmore Girls.
