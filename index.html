I am a second year Computer Science Ph.D. student at Stanford University, advised by Prof. Leo Guibas. I received my Bachelor's degree in Biomedical Engineering, Electrical/Computer Engineering, and Computer Science from Duke University. I am interested in extracting hidden geometric structures that underlie complex data, particularly at the intersection of computer graphics, geometry/structure processing, and content creation.
